Match report and free highlights as Aston Villa show resolve to beat gutsy Luton at Kenilworth Road; Ollie Watkins' first-half double was wiped out by Tahith Chong and Carlton Morris but Lucas Digne's late header secured away win for Unai Emery's Champions League chasers

Liverpool

Liverpool boss Jurgen Klopp has responded to Nottingham Forest supporters’ chant towards Darwin Nunez during Liverpool’s narrow 1-0 victory at the City Ground. The Uruguay international, returning to the squad from the bench, hit the winner late on, glancing home Alexis Mac Allister’s cross in the ninth minute of stoppage time. The striker was introduced to the game on the hour mark, replacing Andy Robertson, and was subjected to chants of “s*** Andy Carroll” from the home support.
